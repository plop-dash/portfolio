About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Kim, a developer with a background in computer science and a love for coding. I have a bachelors in computer science from the University of Utah and am currently pursuing my masters in computer science at Georgia Tech while enrolled in the FastTrack Program.

I've always been fascinated by technology and its potential to solve real-world problems since I was a child. Thinking about how games and web applications work and the logic behind them sparked my interest in programming. This passion led me to study computer science. Seeing my growth and progress from creating simple games like asteroids and snake to more complex full stack applications has been incredibly rewarding.

Since then, I've been continously honing my skills and expanding my knowledge in various programming languages and frameworks through online courses, personal projects, and collaborative work. I enjoy working on projects that challenge me to learn new skills and technologies. When I'm not coding, I can usually be found playing video games and hiking with family and friends.

Recently, I've been working with the following technologies:

  • Spring
  • JavaScript
  • Node.js
  • Java
  • Angular
